IBM Intern Orientation Week 2024

Join us for an exhilarating journey into the world of tech sales at the IBM Intern Orientation Week, tailored for our 2024 Sales Interns! This exclusive event, taking place from May 6th to May 10th, 2024, offers a unique opportunity for you to immerse yourself in IBM. As a new addition to our team, your presence at our downtown office, 16 York St, is essential for this comprehensive, week-long event, designed to equip you with the knowledge and skills necessary for a successful internship. Don't forget to complete your registration to secure your spot in what promises to be an unforgettable kickoff to your career in technology sales!
